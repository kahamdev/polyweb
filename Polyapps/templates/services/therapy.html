HOME BASED PHYSIOTHERAPY

The treatment is totally personal and individualized. Home is the environment of security and emotional belonging that increases the level of effective involvement in recovery process.

It is of great help for those with mobility problems and long term conditions that affects mobility.

We Offer home visit physiotherapy service which can be especially helpful if people are having difficulty with their day to day activities, suffering from balance problems and post-operative patients.

We also manage various conditions which requires physiotherapy intervention.
